Welcome

Welcome to the official website of the Mercer County Missouri Sheriff's Office, located in northern Missouri along the Iowa border. This office serves Mercer County, which is located approximately 100 miles north of Kansas City, MO, and encompasses communities such as Princeton (the county seat), Mercer, South Lineville, Ravanna, Mill Grove, Modena, and others.

Here you can find information regarding the Sheriff's Office staff, policies and procedures, community involvement, services offered, upcoming events, contact details, and links to city, county, state, and federal agencies. The Mercer County Sheriff's Office plays a key role in the safety and well-being of the citizens of Mercer County and takes great pride in this endeavor.

Our History

Mercer County was organized on February 14, 1845, from Grundy County, to comply with constitutional limits on county sizes. It was named for General John F. Mercer, a Revolutionary War officer. The first courthouse was a two-story log structure built in 1847 in Princeton. The current courthouse was constructed around 1898.

Although we are very proud of the rich history of Mercer County and the important role the Office of Sheriff has played in it, the Mercer County Sheriff's Office continues to move forward with the latest innovations in law enforcement technology.
