Victim Services

The Mercer County Sheriff's Office is dedicated to ensuring that victims of crime are treated with fairness, compassion, and dignity. We are committed to providing victims with information regarding their rights and assistance in navigating the criminal justice system.

MOVANS (VINE) Notification System

The Missouri Victim Automated Notification System (MOVANS) allows crime victims and concerned citizens to track the custody status of offenders in Missouri jails and prisons.

You can register to receive automated notifications via phone or email whenever an offender's custody status changes (e.g., release, transfer, or escape).

Victim Rights

Under Missouri law, victims of crime have certain rights, including:

  • The right to be present at all criminal justice proceedings.
  • The right to be informed of and heard at guilty plea hearings, sentencing, and probation revocation hearings.
  • The right to restitution.
  • The right to a speedy disposition of the case.
