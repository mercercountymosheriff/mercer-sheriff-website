Concealed Carry Permits (CCW)

The Mercer County Sheriff's Office processes applications for Concealed Carry Permits (CCW) in accordance with Missouri state statutes. Applications are accepted during normal administrative business hours.

Requirements

To obtain a Concealed Carry Permit, applicants must meet the following criteria:

  • Must be at least 19 years of age (or 18 if a member of the United States Armed Forces).
  • Must be a resident of Mercer County.
  • Must provide a valid Certificate of Qualification (Training Certificate) from a state-approved instructor.
  • Must provide a valid Missouri Driver's License or Non-Driver ID with current Mercer County address.
  • Must pass a criminal background check and fingerprinting process.

Please allow up to 45 days for the processing of all applications.

Fees
